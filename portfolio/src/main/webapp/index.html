Projects I worked on

The most impactful personal project I did is the Telegram bot for high school students that helps students to prepare for their final exams. Bot sends tasks to a user, which they solve and bot immediately checks if the answer is correct. It has a rich task database gathered from the previous exams, so the level of difficulty and the relevance is the best possible. I'm proud that it is used by students here in Ukraine, and despite a tough competition it still has well over 600 active users. The bot is written in Java, uses MySQL as a database and deployed to RaspberryPi in my room :) The link to the bot is @Zno_helper_bot (the interface is in Ukrainian)

During my study at school I liked doing 2D games, I used SFML and C++ for developing. The one I like the most is tetris, you can find on my Github here https://github.com/PaulKovalov/tetrisv2.0. I also have a few unfinished games such as Agario, Pentago, and drag racing simulator.
